Frequently Asked Questions

How does Aktivus handle massive RAW files remotely?

We use a hybrid proxy-first approach. You edit with lightweight, cloud-streamed proxies instantly, while the high-res RAW files sync securely in the background. The final render happens on our cloud nodes, not your machine.

Can we invite external clients for review without them seeing the whole project?

Absolutely. You can generate secure, password-protected review links for specific timelines or assets. Clients get a clean, branded interface to leave frame-specific comments—no account required.

Does it replace Premiere Pro or DaVinci Resolve?

It supercharges them. While Aktivus has a robust web-based editor for quick cuts, we offer deep integration via plugins for Premiere Pro, Final Cut, and Resolve. Sync timelines, markers, and media effortlessly.

What about IP security?

We take it seriously. Aktivus employs AES-256 encryption at rest and in transit, enterprise-grade SSO, and forensic watermarking. Your unreleased content is locked down tighter than industry standards.
