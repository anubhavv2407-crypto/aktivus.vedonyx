Frequently Asked

Is Aktivus entirely cloud-based?

Yes, Aktivus leverages a hybrid architecture. Your heavy rendering is offloaded to our cloud farms, while playback remains lightning-fast locally in your browser or desktop app.

Will it support third-party plugins?

At launch, we will support standard OpenFX plugins. We are also building a native ecosystem for creators to publish their own node-based effects.

When will the Beta officially launch?

The timer above indicates our global rollout date. However, waitlisted users will receive staggered access invites leading up to the main event.
