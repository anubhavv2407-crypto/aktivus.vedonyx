Frequently Asked Questions

How does Aktivus streamline pre-production planning?

Aktivus provides integrated moodboards, interactive script breakdowns, and smart scheduling tools that automatically sync with your production calendar, ensuring the entire crew is aligned before the camera even rolls.

Can we manage raw footage and dailies securely during production?

Yes. Our lightning-fast cloud asset manager allows you to upload dailies directly from the set. Editors can access lightweight proxies instantly, while heavy high-res RAW files sync securely in the background without bottlenecking your connection.

Does the post-production suite handle heavy VFX and color grading?

Absolutely. Aktivus features a true node-based compositing engine and professional HDR color wheels rivaling industry-standard studio software. All of this is powered by our distributed cloud rendering, so your local machine never lags.

How does Aktivus solve production friction?

We provide a unified Review Hub where clients can leave frame-accurate visual annotations directly on the timeline. No more confusing email threads or messy timestamp notes—just seamless, real-time collaboration and one-click approvals.
