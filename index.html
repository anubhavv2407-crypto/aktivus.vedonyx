Frequently Asked Questions

How does Aktivus streamline pre-production planning?

Aktivus provides integrated moodboards, interactive script breakdowns, and smart scheduling tools that automatically sync with your production calendar, ensuring the entire crew is aligned before the camera even rolls.

Can we manage raw footage and dailies securely during production?

Yes. Our lightning-fast cloud ingest engine allows you to upload dailies directly from set. Editors can access lightweight proxies instantly, while heavy high-res RAW files (like ARRIRAW or REDCODE) sync securely in the background.

Does the post-production suite handle heavy VFX and color grading?

Absolutely. Aktivus features a true node-based compositing engine and professional ACES color wheels rivaling industry-standard studio software. All powered by our distributed cloud rendering, ensuring your local machine never lags.

How secure is the cloud storage for unreleased IP?

Security is our priority. We utilize AES-256 military-grade encryption at rest and in transit. Visual watermarking and granular role-based access control (RBAC) ensure your high-value IP never leaks before release.

Does it integrate with standard NLEs like Premiere Pro or DaVinci?

Yes. While Aktivus offers a robust internal suite, we fully support AAF and XML roundtripping, allowing seamless integration with Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, and DaVinci Resolve workflows via our custom panel plugins.
